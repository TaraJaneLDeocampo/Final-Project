What is this webpage about?

This webpage is about Sustainable Development Goals(SDG) of Zero Hunger. Zero Hunger is a global initiative that aims to end world hunger.This webpage includes the causes of zero hunger and ways on how we can lessen or solve this problem. With increasing problems about world hunger, we need to be aware of what causes this to be able to avoid experiencing it. With this, we can also find more ways on how we can help other communities, countries or people who lack food resources.
